This is the first post. I built this site to have a minimal, fast space for writing and sharing ideas.

It's powered by Astro and Tailwind CSS, with a focus on clean typography and zero bloat. No JavaScript bloat, no heavy frameworks — just content.

## What's the plan

I'll be writing about software, design, and the process of building things. Short posts, long posts, tutorials, and thought pieces — whatever feels right.

If you're reading this, thanks for being here. More soon.
